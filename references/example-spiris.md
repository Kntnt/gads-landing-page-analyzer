# Example: Spiris (National SaaS Platform)

This example demonstrates a national SaaS product with specific CTA, visible pricing, and multiple target segments.

## Page Description

The page (spiris.se/ekonomiplattform/bokforing-fakturering) markets Spiris accounting software – a cloud-based financial platform. The page highlights automatic bookkeeping, smart invoicing, receipt management via app, bank integration, payroll, tax filing, and annual reports. Three pricing tiers are presented: Accounting (199 kr/month), Accounting & Invoicing (299 kr/month), and Accounting, Invoicing & Payroll (569 kr/month). New businesses get half price for the first year. The CTA is "Try for free" with secondary CTA "Compare and see price". The page mentions sole proprietorships, limited companies, and associations, but associations are directed to a separate page (/forening). Competitors Bokio and Fortnox are mentioned in the FAQ section. No geographic limitation – the service is national. FAQ explains that Spiris was previously called Visma eEkonomi.
