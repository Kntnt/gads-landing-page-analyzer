# Example: SafeTeam Linköping (Local Service Provider)

This example demonstrates a local service provider with multiple target segments and a broad product page used as a landing page.

## Page Description

The page is primarily about access control systems in Linköping. SafeTeam presents itself as a brand-independent supplier of access control systems and mentions the brands Aptus, ASSA, Axema Vaka, Vanderbilt, RCO and Salto. Under optional add-ons the page highlights intercom systems, laundry room booking, digital locks, code locks, and Passage Online (administration service). The page explicitly targets multiple industries: property companies, housing cooperatives (HOA), retail, construction, industry, offices, healthcare and schools. The CTA is "Get a quote" – relatively generic. The team operates from a regional office in Mjärdevi, Linköping and serves all of Östergötland including Norrköping, Motala, Jönköping, and Örebro.
