# Example: Grown (B2B Consulting – Strategic Transformation)

This example demonstrates a B2B consulting firm with a broad homepage used as a landing page, strategic positioning language, and multiple program offerings.

## Page Description

The page (grown.se) presents Grown as a "strategic transformation partner" – explicitly not an agency, not campaigns, not inbound coaching. The main message is that 73% of B2B buyers have made their choice before talking to sales. The offering is organized into three programs: Revenue Accelerator (8–10 weeks, marked "Recommended"), Alignment Day (one-day workshop, marked "Popular"), and Strategic consulting (ongoing sparring partner for CMO or CRO). The page specifies that Grown suits B2B companies with 30–500 employees selling SaaS, services, or physical products. Founder Magnus Brynestam is personally highlighted in the CTA. 450+ customer logos are shown (including Semcon, Atlas Copco, Carasent). The CTA is "Book a conversation" – 30 minutes with Magnus, no slides, no commitments. Grown is a HubSpot partner (badge in footer, link in nav). Offices in Karlskrona and Stockholm but no geographic limitation. Note: the term "inbound marketing" appears in the page's title tag but not in body text – the page positions itself away from inbound coaching.
